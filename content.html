05 — Licensing

Content Licensing

Included in sale: Elite Solo Class and Medal Classes are transferred with full intellectual property rights. The buyer owns this content outright.
Partner content: All other content packages are from external content partners. The platform integrations, media hosting structure, and content management system are fully included. Content partner licenses must be negotiated separately by the buyer. Existing partner contacts and relationships will be shared during handover to facilitate a smooth transition.

Content Partner Advantages for the Buyer

Ready Infrastructure — All content management, media delivery, and streaming infrastructure is built and operational. Adding or updating content requires no development work.

Established Relationships — Partner contacts and communication history are included in the handover. The buyer does not start from zero.

Proven Catalog Structure — 17 packages with 3,500+ assets across video, audio, and PDF. The content hierarchy and metadata are fully built out in the database.
